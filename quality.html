About the quality team

At the Quality Department, we are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of product quality. Our team advises our Board of Directors on quality management activities, ensuring compliance with leading standards like BRC, FSMA, ISO22000, ISO17025 and others as UEBT, RA, USDA, NOP, SMETA, HALAL, KOSHER.

We streamline processes, conduct rigorous testing, and drive innovation to meet customer demands. Together, we're committed to continuous improvement and excellence across the board.

Laboratory

Chromatography Analyse

We take great pride in the safety and quality of our products. To achieve this, we utilize a sophisticated analytical technique known as chromatography analysis. This department plays a crucial role in safeguarding product integrity by meticulously analyzing samples for the presence of specific parameters, such as pesticides and piperine.

Our dedicated team of chromatography experts utilizes advanced equipment and established protocols to conduct these analyses. They meticulously assess each sample against stringent legal regulations and internal specifications, ensuring complete compliance and the highest quality standards. This rigorous process allows us to identify and address any potential issues promptly, guaranteeing the safety and consistency of every product we deliver.

Laboratory

Microbiological Analyse

Our unwavering commitment to product safety and quality extends to the implementation of a comprehensive microbiological analysis program. This department plays a critical role in ensuring the adherence to stringent legal regulations and established quality specifications. Our highly qualified and experienced team of microbiologists utilizes cutting-edge technology and standardized methodologies to meticulously analyze various microbiological parameters, including Total Plate Count (TPC), Yeast & Mold, Coliforms, E. coli, Salmonella. This rigorous approach enables the early detection and mitigation of potential microbiological hazards, guaranteeing the production of safe and high-quality products that meet all regulatory requirements and customer expectations.

Laboratory

Physical Chemical Analyse

The department's meticulous procedures involve the meticulous evaluation of a broad spectrum of parameters, including but not limited to: SVO (Steam Volatile Oils), Moisture Content, Density, Particle size, Foreign Matter, Coumarin Content, Insect fragment .v.v.

These analyses are meticulously conducted in strict accordance with established legal requirements and industry-specific specifications. By rigorously evaluating these critical parameters, our Physical and Chemical Analysis department plays an instrumental role in ensuring the consistent quality, safety, and compliance of our products. In particular, we have developed and used NIRS technology to control SVO for spice. This is a breakthrough in testing that helps us assess more accurately and faster and ensure the batch homogenized.
