Cassia

Pioneering the forefront in Vietnamese cassia, propel your brand forward with the purest essence of Vietnam's finest spice

Harvested from evergreen trees thriving in mountainous terrains at elevations spanning 300 to 1,700 meters above sea level, this spice is meticulously derived from the inner bark. Celebrated for its unique aromatic and flavorful properties, these attributes emanate from the elevated concentration of essential oils. Maintaining unwavering consistency through our well-established global supply chains, we proudly offer Vietnam's premium cassia with a proven track record. Boasting an annual yield exceeding 15,000 metric tons and a robust cassia stronghold of 6,000 tons, our commitment remains steadfast, ensuring a reliable and consistent supply.

Origin: Vietnam, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, China.

Our range of products

Our reddish to light brown cassia delight is microbiologically guaranteed, leading the way in eliminating 100% of foreign flavors and odors. With over three decades of expertise in the spice industry, we offer a diverse range of products customized to meet our customers' needs.

Stick

Hand-sourced from sustainable Vietnamese farms, these fragrant quills boast a complex sweetness and subtle heat, ideal for simmering, baking, and brewing.

Split

An economical choice for you to enjoy this comfortable herbal and aromatic scent, harvested at the peak of freshness.

Square Cut

Caters to your precise specifications. Particle size can be finely tuned to meet your unique requirements and made to order.

Tea Bag Cut

Skip the prep; embrace the pleasure. Savor the warmth and aroma of local cassaia in handy tea bag form. Perfect for tea bad packing for brewing moments of bliss.

Broken & Clean

Broken for bold infusion, Clean for delicate artistry. Unlocks a nuanced sweetness and subtle heat, promising to elevate your creations with a touch of pure elegance.

Cut & Sifted

Blend the boldness of cut with the finesse of sift to create depth, dimension, and an unforgettable flavor journey.

Whole Tube

Unblemished bark, grown with sustainable practices. These majestic tubes are perfect for steeping in broths, curries, and tea.
