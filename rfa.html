RFA Certified Products

Son Ha is dedicated to establishing and adhering to rigorous sustainability standards. We collaborate closely with our farming community to turn this commitment into a reality because we firmly believe that nothing is more important than fostering sustainability. In alignment with our customers' initiatives to achieve full sustainability in their sourcing activities, we have embarked on this journey with them.

As a key component of our sustainability program, we proudly offer Rainforest Alliance (RFA) certified products in Cassia and Black Pepper, aligning with the Union for Ethical Bio Trade (UEBT). It's worth noting that Son Ha is among the pioneers in Vietnam to attain RFA certification for Cassia, showcasing our leadership in sustainable practices.

Origin: Vietnam
