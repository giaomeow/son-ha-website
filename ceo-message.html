KEIJI TANIGUCHI
CEO of Son Ha Spice & Flavorings

Pacific Basin (Son Ha Spice & Flavoring Co.,) takes immense pride in its position as a leading spice exporter in Vietnam. Since our humble beginning in 1992, we have dedicated ourselves to supplying high-value spices to customers worldwide. Our unwavering commitment to quality and compliance, coupled with a steadfast focus on the safety of our customers and employees, has been the cornerstone of our policy.

In our pursuit of excellence, we continuously strive to provide the best quality products and services to our customers. Simultaneously, we are dedicated to initiatives in backward integration, striving to turn the concept of 'farm to table' into a reality. Rigorous quality controls govern the careful sourcing of our raw materials from their origins, meeting the exacting quality requirements of our customers.

As a subsidiary of Mitani Corporation (Listed Public Company in Japan) since 2017, we have invested significantly in our manufacturing facilities and quality control laboratory (ISO17025), positioning ourselves to meet the evolving challenges of the global food industry. Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our strengths in offering Organic and Rain Forest Alliance certified products, etc. We are delighted to collaborate with our customers, contributing to a more sustainable world for future generations.

Recognizing our responsibilities to supply chain, particularly to the farming communities integral to our success, we actively engage in Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) activities. These initiatives focus on improving their livelihood, healthcare, education, and empowering women. We express our deep gratitude to our customers for their unwavering support in these endeavors.

In our global journey to create delighted customers, we acknowledge the invaluable contribution of our employees - the pillar of strength in our organization. We continually enhance the quality of the working environment, prioritizing a harmonious work-life balance for all in the Pacific Basin family. Our success story shall be intricately woven with the dedication of our employees, and we remain committed to making Pacific Basin a great place to work.

Embracing a culture of continuous improvement, our Kaizen initiatives drive positive changes every day. We are steadfast in our commitment to be a preferred supplier of value-added spice ingredients to our esteemed customers. As we forge ahead, Pacific Basin remains dedicated to bringing positive transformations and upholding the highest standards in spice industry.


KEIJI TANIGUCHI
