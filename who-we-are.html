With a humble beginning in 1994 as a cassia processing facility in Northern Vietnam, Son Ha Spice & Flavourings has evolved into a premier exporter of top-quality spices from Vietnam. Our journey has been marked by transformation and growth, as we have continually upgraded our manufacturing facilities and expanded our product range to become one of the foremost players in the industry.

Under the stewardship of Mitani Corporation, a respected Public Listed Company in Japan, we have further solidified our position, drawing on their rich legacy to propel us to new heights. Today, we boast a diverse portfolio, encompassing sought-after spices such as Black Pepper, Star Anise, Ginger, and Turmeric.

Our commitment extends beyond product excellence; we are dedicated to meeting the unique needs of our esteemed customers across various sectors including seasonings, condiments, bakery, confectionery, tea, retail, and supermarkets. By prioritizing quality and collaboration, we strive to be the preferred partner in our customers' quest for the finest ingredients.
