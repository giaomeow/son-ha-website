MISSION AND VISION

Make a difference by creating value from sustainable utilization of precious forest resources

Revitalize your brand with conscious choices. Son Ha transcends the realm of spices, building trust through sustainable practices and transparency. Partner with us, the leading Vietnamese manufacturer, to secure a competitive advantage in a market where consumers prioritize authenticity and value. Each ingredient shares a story of integrity, enriching your brand and positively impacting communities. Our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and compliance ensures you can savor our products with complete confidence.

We believe in a future where respect for the land and its people is the key factor. Our farmers are not just suppliers, they are partners in a shared vision of a vibrant landscape. We go beyond sourcing and invest in the well-being of our farming communities through education, healthcare, and financial support. This empowers them, sustains their skills, and strengthens our long-term partnerships. Join us in a movement where taste meets purpose.

 Innovation goes beyond mere efficiency; it is the gateway to a brighter future for both spices and the planet. Our dedication extends beyond enhancing machinery for sharper end-of-line detection; it encompasses the meticulous assurance that our sustainable products flawlessly align with the highest international standards.

Taste commences with trust. Our ingredients are meticulously selected from reliable, credible partners. Subsequently, we lavish them with care in our state-of-the-art processing lines. Drawing on over two decades of expertise, we skillfully convert these natural treasures into enriched masterpieces, poised to spark culinary adventures across the globe.

Beyond the role of mere spice merchants, we are dedicated artisans, meticulously crafting masterpieces from the rich bounty of Vietnam. Our commitment extends from nurturing the seeds to the detailed processing of each product. With over three decades of unwavering dedication, we have honed our skills, enabling us to create value-added components that stand as a testament to our passion and expertise. Join us on a journey guided by Son Ha, where each product becomes a celebration of the world’s finest spices.

Collaboratively, we shape a fabric of sustainability. Son Ha extends an invitation to join us in fostering the environment through initiatives like forest preservation and biodiversity training programs. Together, let's cultivate a future where every product reflects the harmony of nature and responsible growth. Our commitment to sustainability is ongoing and perpetual.

Minimize Your Risks, Maximize Your Potential

Beyond mere spices, it's a global culinary journey. Allow our proficiency in sourcing and exporting to infuse your brand with endless possibilities. Son Ha boasts a dedicated 30,000-square-foot BRC A+ certified facility along with seven warehouses, ensuring unparalleled quality control at every step. Our QC/QC lab is accredited to ISO 17025.Our in-house third-party validated steam sterilizers, metal detection in cleaning and milling lines, X-ray detection system and an internal micro lab are meticulously designed to earn your trust and ensure the best of quality.
