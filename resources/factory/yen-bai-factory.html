Yen Bai Factory

Situated within the core of the most extensive Cassia cultivation region in Vietnam, approximately 175 kilometers from Hanoi, the Yen Bai processing branch maintains a substantial operational footprint encompassing 3 hectares. This strategically located facility serves as the primary sourcing and pre-processing hub for Cassia/Cinnamon, supporting the livelihoods of over 200 workers within the local farming community.
